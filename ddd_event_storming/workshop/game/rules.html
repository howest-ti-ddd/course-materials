Win Condition

The team "wins" if, by the end, you have:

  • A coherent timeline of Domain Events with Actors, Systems, and Bounded Contexts.
  • One process modeled with Policies and Commands.
  • A software design slice with Aggregates and their Commands and Events.
  • A list of prioritized problems and next steps.

Perfection is not required; shared understanding is.

Setup (10 minutes)

Team Composition

  • 1 Moderator
  • Up to 9 Domain Experts
  • Up to 9 Developers

Minimum to play: 1 moderator + 4 participants.

Materials

  • Wall / long surface & Sharpies
  • Sticky notes in all required colors
  • Painter‚Äôs tape for chapters/boundaries
  • Timer visible to all

Role Assignment

  • Hand each student a role card or let them choose randomly.
  • Every role storms fully (adds events, speaks up) plus uses their superpower at key moments.
  • Missing role? When a role-specific duty comes up, the moderator first asks for a volunteer; if no one steps in, the moderator either covers it personally or assigns someone.

Session Timeline (3 hours)

0. Setup & Brief (10 min)

  • Reread this Rulebook (2 min).
  • Moderator explains color legend & win condition (3 min).
  • Everyone skims "Het Verhaal van de App" (5 min).

1. Big Picture Event Storming (55 min)

  • Chaotic Exploration (15 min): Write Events and Hotspots.
  • Enforce Timeline (20 min): Order events left-to-right, mark chapters.
  • Actors & Systems (10 min): Add Actors, Systems, and swimlanes.
  • Walkthroughs (10 min): Narrate forward then reverse. After this, only the Story Anchor can consult the story.
  • Archivist Unlock: After walkthrough, Archivist proposes missing events.

2. Problems & Priorities (10 min)

  • Add/cluster Hotspots & Opportunities. Arrow-vote to prioritize.

3. Bounded Context Discovery (15 min)

The Boundary Builder leads using the Bounded Contexts page. Sketch tentative boundaries (dotted lines), name contexts; add notes for translations/ownership.

Process (step-by-step)
  • Gather attention (1 min)
  • Walk the decision tree (10 min)
  • Quick team validation (3 min)

4. Process Modelling (40 min)

  • Choose a slice, map the Happy Path with Events.
  • Apply Grammar: Event ‚Üí Policy ‚Üí Command.
  • Model Non-Happy Paths and conduct a backwards walkthrough.
  • Repeat until satisfied, for another scenario or time runs out.

5. Software Design Slice (35 min)

  • Replace internal Systems with Aggregates where you own invariants.
  • For each Aggregate, list the Commands it handles and Events it emits.
  • Refine names for a consistent ubiquitous language.
  • Got through a new Bounded Context Discovery, see: 3. Bounded Context Discovery.

6. Wrap Up & Reflection (15 min)

  • Photograph boards to preserve the session outcomes.
  • Collect Exit Tickets: ‚ÄúOne thing on the board that wouldn‚Äôt exist without my role.‚Äù
