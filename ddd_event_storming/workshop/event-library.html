App & User Lifecycle

  • App Discovered
  • App Downloaded
  • Account Created (via email or social media)
  • Catalog Browsed (in Guest Mode)
  • Location Permission Requested
  • Location Permission Granted (e.g., Always, While Using, Never)
  • Location Permission Denied
  • Permissions Adjusted
  • App Opened

Observation & Data Capture

  • Bird Sighted
  • Observation Data Registered (including time, GPS location, user ID)
  • Observation Stored Offline
  • Photo Captured
  • Sound Fragment Recorded

Data Synchronization

  • Observation Synchronized
  • Upload Attempted
  • Upload Failed Notification Received
  • Upload Retried
  • Data Uploaded

AI & Identification

  • Photo Sent to AI Service
  • AI Suggestion Received
  • Probable Species Identified by AI (score > 90%)
  • AI Alternatives Suggested (score 60-90%)
  • Community Help Suggested (AI score < 60% or not a bird)

User Actions & Social

  • AI Suggestion Accepted
  • AI Suggestion Rejected
  • Species Manually Selected
  • Observation Validated by Expert
  • Observation Locked (after expert validation)
  • Observation Shared
  • Observation Set to Private
  • Observation Shared with Followers
  • Observation Made Public
  • Location Blurred (for rare species)
  • Precise Location Shared (for rare species)
  • Observation Liked
  • Comment Added
  • Observation Status Changed (e.g., to Approved/Validated)
  • Observation Sent for Review (due to conflict)
  • Review Decision Reached

Rewards & Payments

  • Lifelist Updated
  • Badge Earned
  • Badge Awarded Notification Received
  • Payment Processed (via App Store/Google Play)
  • Subscription Expired
  • Premium Features Blocked

System Updates & Integrations

  • Bird Catalog Updated (by central team)
  • Bird Species Split
  • Lifelist Recalculated
  • Bird Update Notification Received
  • Validated Observations Exported to Scientific Database
  • Data Sharing License Agreed
