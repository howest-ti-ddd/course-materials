Deployment: Everything goes live together

Examples: Traditional web applications, many enterprise systems

Definition: Multiple separate deployment units that communicate over a network.

Communication: Network protocols (HTTP, gRPC, messaging)

Examples: Microservices, serverless architectures, SOA

The choice between monolithic and distributed is a fundamental architectural decision that affects all other choices.
