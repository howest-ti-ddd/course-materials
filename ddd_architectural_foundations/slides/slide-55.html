Microservices: The Big Picture

Goal

To achieve maximum team autonomy, independent scalability, and deployment velocity for large, complex systems by breaking them into fine-grained services.

Usecase

A large e-commerce platform where the shopping cart, recommendations, and payment processing are all separate microservices that can be scaled and deployed independently.

Teams

This architecture *requires* small, autonomous, Stream-aligned teams that own their services end-to-end (Inverse Conway Maneuver).
