Event-Driven: The Big Picture

Goal

To achieve extreme decoupling, scalability, and resilience by allowing services to communicate asynchronously through events, without direct knowledge of one another.

Usecase

A social media platform. When a `PhotoPosted` event occurs, notification, timeline, and analytics services all react independently without being coupled.

Teams

Strongly favors Stream-aligned teams owning workflows. Platform teams are crucial for managing the complex event broker infrastructure.
