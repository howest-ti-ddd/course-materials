Event-Driven: Trade-offs

When to Use

  • For highly scalable and resilient systems.
  • When you need to decouple different parts of a large system.
  • For parallel, long-running workflows (e.g., video processing).
  • When you want to easily add new functionality without changing existing services.

When Not to Use

  • Simple request-response interactions.
  • When you need atomic transactions across multiple services.
  • Debugging can be complex due to the asynchronous flow.
