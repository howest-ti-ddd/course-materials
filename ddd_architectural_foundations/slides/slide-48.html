Pipeline: The Big Picture

Goal

To handle simple, one-way data processing tasks by breaking the process into a series of discrete, single-purpose, and reusable steps (filters).

Usecase

An ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) tool. Data is extracted (producer), transformed through various steps (transformer filters), and loaded into a destination (consumer).

Teams

Very flexible. The clear separation of concerns in each filter works well with all team topologies, from Stream-aligned to Complicated-subsystem.
