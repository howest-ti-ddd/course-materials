Key Takeaways

  • DDD is Logical: It's your blueprint for understanding the business domain.
  • Architecture is Physical: It's how you build and deploy that blueprint.
  • Context is King: Your architecture must fit your specific business needs, team structure, and required "-ilities".
  • It's All About Trade-offs: You can't have everything. Choose your architectural characteristics wisely.
