Pipeline: DDD Fit & Pitfalls

DDD Readiness: Context-Dependent

Not a good fit for modeling an entire Bounded Context.

However, it can be an excellent implementation for a specific process or workflow within a Bounded Context.

Common Anti-Patterns

  • Fat Filters: A single filter does too much work instead of being broken into smaller steps.
  • Bi-directional Pipes: Breaking the one-way flow principle.
