Protect Your Architecture!

Prevent architectural drift with automated Fitness Functions.

What are they?

Tests that run with your CI/CD pipeline to verify architectural rules.

Why use them?

To provide a safety net, allowing the system to evolve without violating key principles.

Example Rule

"The 'Orders' module should NOT directly depend on the 'Shipping' module."

layeredArchitecture()
    .layer("Controller").definedBy("..controller..")
    .layer("Service").definedBy("..service..")
    .layer("Persistence").definedBy("..persistence..")
 
    .whereLayer("Controller").mayNotBeAccessedByAnyLayer()
    .whereLayer("Service").mayOnlyBeAccessedByLayers("Controller")
    .whereLayer("Persistence").mayOnlyBeAccessedByLayers("Service");

An ArchUnit fitness function for a Layered Architecture.
