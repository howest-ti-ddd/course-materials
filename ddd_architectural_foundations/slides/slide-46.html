Service-Based: DDD Fit & Pitfalls

DDD Readiness: Very Good Fit

This is a natural fit. Each coarse-grained domain service can be a direct implementation of a Bounded Context, providing clear, physically separate boundaries.

Common Anti-Patterns

  • Chatty Services: Too much direct communication between services, indicating poor domain boundaries.
  • The Shared Database Trap: Failing to logically partition the shared DB, meaning a table change breaks multiple services.
