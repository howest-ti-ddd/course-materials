Distributed Architecture: Deep Dive

Benefits

  • Independent deployability of components
  • Can scale individual services based on demand
  • Technology diversity (different stacks per service)
  • Better fault isolation

Challenges

  • Increased complexity in deployment and operations
  • Network communication overhead and latency
  • Difficult distributed debugging
  • Must handle network failures and eventual consistency

Distributed systems are inherently more complex, but provide flexibility at scale that monoliths cannot match.
