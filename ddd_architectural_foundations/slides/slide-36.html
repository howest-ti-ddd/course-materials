Modular Monolith: The Big Picture

Goal

To achieve high modularity and domain partitioning within a single deployment unit, balancing development agility with operational simplicity.

Usecase

A small restaurant delivery system (`EasyMeals`) that needs clear domain separation (Orders, Payments, Inventory) but doesn't require the complexity of a distributed system.

Teams

Works best with domain-aligned, cross-functional teams (Stream-aligned) that can own a business feature from UI to database within a single module.
