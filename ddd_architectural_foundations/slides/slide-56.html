Microservices: Trade-offs

When to Use (The Benefits)

  • For very large, complex systems.
  • When you need high, independent scalability for different parts of the app.
  • Enables multiple teams to work and deploy independently at high velocity.
  • Allows using different technology stacks for different services.

When Not to Use (The Costs)

  • High operational complexity (deployment, monitoring, networking).
  • Distributed data management is very hard (eventual consistency).
  • The Fallacies of Distributed Computing are a constant concern.
  • Not a good choice for small teams or simple applications.
