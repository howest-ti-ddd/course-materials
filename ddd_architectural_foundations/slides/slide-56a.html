Microservices: DDD Fit & Pitfalls

DDD Readiness: Excellent Fit

Microservices can be a perfect physical implementation of DDD's Bounded Contexts. Each service has complete autonomy over its domain model and persistence.

Common Anti-Patterns

  • Anemic Micro-Services: Creating tiny services around data entities instead of business capabilities.
  • Distributed Monolith: Services with tangled dependencies that must be deployed together.
  • Domain Model Leakage: Sharing domain models across service boundaries instead of translating at boundaries.
