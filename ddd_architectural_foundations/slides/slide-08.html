DDD Recap: Domain Model

What is it?

A representation of the business domain in code. It captures business rules, behaviors, and relationships - not just data.

Rich vs Anemic:

Rich models have behavior, anemic models are just data containers.

❌ Anemic: order.total = 100

✅ Rich: order.calculateTotal()

Example: Order Domain Model

The domain model captures what the business does, not just what data it stores.
