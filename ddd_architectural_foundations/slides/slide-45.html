Service-Based: Trade-offs

When to Use

  • As a target architecture when migrating from a large monolith.
  • When you need independent deployment for different business areas.
  • For benefits of distributed systems without the high complexity of microservices.

When Not to Use

  • When fine-grained, independent scalability is required.
  • If a shared database (a single point of failure) is unacceptable.
  • For very large systems where a shared database would be a bottleneck.
