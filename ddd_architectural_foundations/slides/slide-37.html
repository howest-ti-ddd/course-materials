Modular Monolith: Trade-offs

When to Use

  • The default choice for most new applications.
  • Small to medium-sized systems.
  • Projects with tight budget and time constraints.
  • When you want strong domain boundaries (DDD) without distributed complexity.

When Not to Use

  • Systems requiring high, independent scalability.
  • Large, globally distributed teams needing independent deployments.
  • When different parts of the system need different tech stacks.
