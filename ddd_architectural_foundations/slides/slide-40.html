Microkernel: The Big Picture

Goal

To create a highly extensible and adaptable system by isolating volatile or custom code into independent plug-ins, keeping the core stable.

Usecase

An insurance claims system where the rules for each state are in separate plug-ins. A rule change in one state doesn't require re-deploying the entire system.

Teams

Well-suited for Enabling teams (who can experiment in plug-ins) and Complicated-subsystem teams (for specialized plug-ins like analytics).
