Service-Based: The Big Picture

Goal

To gain benefits of a distributed architecture (like independent deployment) without the high complexity of microservices, by using a few coarse-grained services.

Usecase

An electronics recycling system (`Going Green`) where `Quoting`, `Assessment`, and `Recycling` are separate services that can be deployed independently.

Teams

Works best with Stream-aligned teams focused on a specific domain service. Becomes challenging if workflows cross service boundaries frequently.
