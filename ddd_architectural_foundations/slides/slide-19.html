Monolithic Architecture: Deep Dive

Benefits

  • Simpler development experience
  • Easier to debug (everything in one place)
  • No network overhead between components
  • Simpler deployment and testing

Challenges

  • Can become a "Big Ball of Mud" over time
  • Must deploy the entire application for any change
  • Scaling is all-or-nothing
  • Technology stack decisions apply to the whole system

Good monoliths are modular - they maintain clean boundaries internally even though they deploy as a unit.
